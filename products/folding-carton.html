Light as a feather

With a refined feel that is excellent for retails shelves, our folding cartons are made using 16pt lightweight SBS paperboard. Cartons can hold up to 1 lb but don't like to go it alone. These can be perfectly paired with one of our mailer or shipping boxes if you need to drop them in the mail.
